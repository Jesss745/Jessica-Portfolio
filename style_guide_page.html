Fonts

Bebas Neue is used for Headings

Bebas Neue is suitable for headlines. This font stands out with its weight and unique style of all capitalizatoon which allows it to stand out.

Montserrat used for Smaller Headings and Body Text

Montserrat is mainly seen in websites and online media. With its readability and ease of scaling, Montserrat is a simple and widely available typeface that matches Bebas Neue.
