Fonts

Montserrat used for Headings

Montserrat is mainly seen in websites and online media. With its readability and ease of scaling, Montserrat is a simple and widely available typeface that matches Open Sans .

Open Sans is used for Smaller Headings and Body Text

Open Sans embodies simplicity and provides clarity to text. Through its well-balanced letterforms maintain consistency across different weights and styles, ensuring a harmonious and modern appearance.
